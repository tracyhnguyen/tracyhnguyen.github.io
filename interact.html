Public Relations Chair

August 2019 - June 2020

As the club Public Relations Chair, I create digital flyers to promote events, fundraisers, meetings and much more. Examples can be found here. In addition, I also create meeting slides for every meeting and help present along with other board members.

I also run the club's social media accounts: Facebook Page (@LQHSInteract), Facebook Group (La Quinta Interact Club), Instagram (@lqinteract) and also created the club website (LQHS Interact Club Website)

In addition, I helped in finding service event opportunities for the club two times a month by contacting and cooperating with event coordinators outside of school. I help organize the list of members attending as well as finding transportation to those who need rides.

Doubles Badminton Tournament

January 2019 - March 2020

Our club hosted the school's first ever Doubles Badminton Tournament. Games were held every Tuesday, Thursday, and Friday during lunch where 9 games were held simultaneously each game day. There was a total of 86 teams (172 students). The tournament was ongoing for three months until COVID-19 cut it short at Round 4. The '19 - '20 Badminton Tournament Webpage has all the info such as game schedules, rules, and pictures of the tournament.

Tasks I did included:

  • Attending every game day
  • Regularly keeping track of teams in Winners and Losers Bracket
  • Checking-in teams and recording their scores at every game
  • Organizing each Round and matching teams against each other by difficulty level (beginner, intermediate, expert)
  • Communicating with teams to schedule/reschedule game days
  • Helping referee games
  • Creating and regularly updating the Badminton Tournament Webpage
